HOW IT WORKS
Step 1
Build a custom profile tailored to your music tastes. Start from scratch or connect your account with Spotify or Apple Music and let us do the work for you.
Step 2
Pick a plan. We offer a range of plans to suit a variety of budgets and vinyl lovers. Billed on a monthly basis so you can change it up as needed.
Step 3
Your vinyl is shipped directly to you. All you have to do is put it on the turntable and drop the needle down. Sit back, get lost in the sounds you love.
